The Creator

Aria Thorne

Aria Thorne is an avant-garde digital artisan and jeweler based out of Copenhagen. Specializing in computational aesthetics, she blurs the boundaries of physical wearability and immersive spatial representations.

Her projects combine generative mathematical patterns with classic metalsmithing techniques, rendering pieces as high-fidelity interactive digital sculptures before bringing them to physical form.
